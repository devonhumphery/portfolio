About

👤
Devon Humphery
MA, Emerging Media Design & Development
Ball State University, 2026

I work where research, design, and policy meet.

I hold an MA in Emerging Media Design and Development (HCI concentration) from Ball State University, where I spent two years studying how people interact with technology, systems, and stories. Before that, a BS in General Studies with a Theatre minor gave me a foundation in empathy, communication, and audience.

My work spans usability testing, interactive narrative development, transmedia storytelling, technical Python development, and government affairs. I've coded games, evaluated AI chatbots, designed museum exhibits, built transmedia campaigns, and supported a $44B state budget session — sometimes in the same semester.

Currently serving as a Congressional Defense and Tech Policy Intern in the office of U.S. Senator Jim Banks in Washington, D.C. Verizon Scholar — Indiana General Assembly Intern of the Year, 2025.
